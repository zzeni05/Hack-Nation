# Lab Facility Constraints

The lab has one luminescence-capable plate reader and one CryoMed controlled-rate freezer. Both should be reserved at least two weeks before the freezing run.

Liquid nitrogen transfer is available Monday through Friday before 3 PM. Weekend transfer requires facility approval.

The plate reader supports white opaque 96-well plates for luminescent viability assays. Black plates are reserved for fluorescence assays and should not be used for CellTiter-Glo.
