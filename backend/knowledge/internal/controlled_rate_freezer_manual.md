# Controlled-Rate Freezer Manual

The lab has one CryoMed controlled-rate freezer on Bench 4. The validated mammalian-cell profile is hold at 4C, cool at -1C per minute to -80C, and hold until transfer. A full run takes approximately 90 minutes.

The freezer must be reserved in the equipment calendar before cell harvest. Do not interrupt a run once started. If the CryoMed unit is unavailable, use Mr. Frosty containers at -80C as a backup and record the deviation.
