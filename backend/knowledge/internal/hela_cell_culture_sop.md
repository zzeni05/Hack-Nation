# HeLa Cell Culture SOP

## Routine maintenance

Maintain HeLa cells in DMEM high glucose supplemented with 10% fetal bovine serum and 1% penicillin-streptomycin. Incubate cultures at 37C in a humidified 5% CO2 incubator.

Passage cells when they reach 70-80% confluence. Aspirate medium, rinse once with sterile PBS, add 0.25% trypsin-EDTA, and incubate until cells detach. Neutralize trypsin with complete medium and transfer cells to a conical tube.

## Expansion for experimental runs

For cryopreservation studies, use cultures below passage 20 where possible. Expand into T-75 or T-175 vessels so that all experimental groups are harvested from comparable passage number and confluence. Record passage number, confluence, medium lot, serum lot, and incubator ID in the run notes.
