# Viability Assay Runbook

## Default viability method

The default post-thaw viability method is trypan blue exclusion. Mix cell suspension 1:1 with 0.4% trypan blue and count immediately. Record live cells, dead cells, total cells, and percent viability.

## Comparative viability studies

For experiments comparing cryoprotectants, media formulations, or recovery conditions, a plate-reader assay is preferred when the goal is comparison across multiple timepoints or conditions. CellTiter-Glo may be used as the primary endpoint when luminescence plate reader access is available. Plate 1e4 cells per well in white opaque 96-well plates and read luminescence at 0h, 24h, and 48h post-thaw.

Use trypan blue as a secondary orthogonal readout for membrane integrity.
